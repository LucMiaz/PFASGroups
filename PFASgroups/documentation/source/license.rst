Data Attribution
----------------

OECD PFAS Classifications
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

The OECD-defined PFAS groups (IDs 1-28) are based on classifications from the
Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD).

Source: `OECD Portal on Per and Poly Fluorinated Chemicals <https://www.oecd.org/chemicalsafety/portal-perfluorinated-chemicals/>`_

EPA CompTox
^^^^^^^^^^^

Some validation data is derived from the EPA CompTox Chemicals Dashboard.

Source: `CompTox Chemicals Dashboard <https://comptox.epa.gov/dashboard>`_
